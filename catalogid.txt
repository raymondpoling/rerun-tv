12 characters.

First 5 is upcased first five letters that are not part of: the, of, a, in.

Second 2 are disambiguation numbers (IE, if the first five matches another series,
then this is used to differentiate). Example:
House             - HOUSE00
House on the Hill - HOUSE01

The serial number is first come first serve.

The next 2 numbers are the season number.

The next 3 numbers is the episode number.

So, House on the Hill Season 2 Episode 5 would be:
HOUSE0102005
